Frecuently Asked Questions

User FAQ's

What is Party App

+

It is a tool that provides you with information on leisure venues "Hotels, restaurants, nightclubs, etc." with the aim of facilitating this information to users and promoting all businesses of the same type that are registered in the application.

Can I find locations in other cities?

+

Yes, party app provides information on all registered businesses around the world, allowing the user to choose which restaurant is of interest.

Do they offer promotions or discounts for being a Party App user?

+

We do not currently provide this service; however, with the great acceptance of Party App, we expect to be able to negotiate participation in a dynamic of promotions and discounts for Party App users.

Can reservations be made directly through the app?

+

We do not currently provide this service; however, this feature is intended for the future and to provide greater convenience to users and businesses registered on the party app.

Business FAQ's

How can I promote my business on the app?

+

Currently, advertising for Party App is done manually, so in the contact section you must send a quote request which will be sent directly to the email address provided.

Is there a registration fee?

+

Business registration and account creation is completely free.

How long does registration take?

+

Business registrations are immediate once the registration form is completed; however, in case of any breach of our terms and conditions, we reserve the right to delete the registration.

How can I find out how many users have viewed my business on the app?

+

Party App has a built-in click counter, so that every person searching for a place is counted, providing businesses with a more realistic popularity metric.

Payment and billing methods

+

Currently, Party App does not have an automated payment system, so all payments to Party App must be made via bank transfer.
Account details will be provided to users who wish to contract Party App's advertising services via email.
